# Venice AI Assistant - User Guide

Welcome to the Venice AI Assistant! This guide is designed to help you get the most out of your new AI companion. Whether you're a complete beginner or a tech-savvy pro, we'll walk you through everything step-by-step.

## 1. What is this extension?
Imagine having a super-smart assistant living right inside your web browser. The Venice AI Assistant is a Chrome Extension that opens a handy sidebar next to whatever website you're looking at. It can read the page you're on, summarize long articles, watch YouTube videos for you (by reading the transcript), generate beautiful images, and even speak answers out loud! It uses the powerful Venice AI to do all of this securely and privately.

## 2. Step-by-Step Installation Guide
Follow these simple steps to get your assistant up and running:

1. **Download the Extension**: Make sure you have the Venice AI Assistant folder downloaded on your computer.
2. **Open Chrome Extensions**: Open your Google Chrome browser, type `chrome://extensions/` into the address bar at the top, and press Enter.
3. **Enable Developer Mode**: Look at the top right corner of the page and turn on the switch that says **Developer mode**.
4. **Load Unpacked**: Click the **Load unpacked** button that appears in the top left. Select the Venice AI Assistant folder from your computer.
5. **Pin the Extension**: Click the puzzle piece icon (🧩) in the top right of Chrome, find the Venice AI Assistant, and click the pin icon (📌) so it's always visible.
6. **Add Your API Key**: 
   - Click the Venice icon or press `Ctrl+Shift+Y` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+Y` (Mac) to open the sidebar.
   - Click the **Settings** gear icon (⚙️).
   - Paste your Venice API Key (you can get this from your account on the Venice AI website) into the box and click Save.

## 3. A Detailed Walkthrough of the UI
Let's take a tour of your new assistant's interface!

### The Main Chat Area
- **Chat Window**: This is the large area where you talk to the AI. Your messages appear on the right, and the AI's answers appear on the left.
- **Message Box**: Type your questions or instructions here at the bottom.
- **Send Button**: Click the arrow button (or press Enter on your keyboard) to send your message.

### The Top Menu Bar
- **New Chat Button (➕)**: Clears the screen and starts a fresh, blank conversation.
- **History Button (🕒)**: Lets you look back at your past conversations and pick up where you left off.
- **Settings Button (⚙️)**: Where you put your API key, choose which AI "brain" (model) you want to use, and change the look of the extension.

### Special Features (Buttons below the chat box)
- **Include Page Context (📄)**: Click this to let the AI "read" the website you are currently looking at. It's perfect for summarizing articles or analyzing tweets!
- **Multi-Tab Context (📑)**: Want the AI to read *several* tabs at once? Use this to select multiple open websites for the AI to look at simultaneously.
- **Upload PDF (📎)**: Click the paperclip to upload a PDF document from your computer so the AI can read and answer questions about it.
- **Generate Image (🎨)**: Ask the AI to draw a picture, then click this button instead of the normal Send button.
- **Text-to-Speech (🔊)**: After the AI answers, click the speaker icon next to its message to hear it spoken out loud.
- **Prompt Chains (🔗)**: This is an advanced feature that lets you set up a multi-step task (like "Read this, then summarize it, then translate it").

## 4. Common Use Cases (With Concrete Examples)

### Example 1: Summarizing a Long Article or YouTube Video
* **The Goal**: You found a 20-minute YouTube video or a massive news article, and you just want the main points without spending all that time.
* **How to do it**:
  1. Open the YouTube video or article in your browser.
  2. Open the Venice sidebar (`Ctrl+Shift+Y` or `Cmd+Shift+Y`).
  3. Click the **Include Page Context (📄)** button so it turns on.
  4. Type: *"Please give me a 3-bullet point summary of this."*
  5. Hit Send! The AI will read the transcript or article and give you the short version.

### Example 2: Generating an Image
* **The Goal**: You need a picture of a cute cat wearing a tiny astronaut helmet for a presentation.
* **How to do it**:
  1. Open the sidebar.
  2. Type: *"A cute fluffy orange cat wearing a shiny astronaut helmet, floating in space."*
  3. Click the **Generate Image (🎨)** button (do not just press Enter).
  4. Wait a few seconds, and your masterpiece will appear!

### Example 3: Using Prompt Chains (Advanced)
* **The Goal**: You want to research a topic, summarize it, and translate it to Spanish, all automatically in one go.
* **How to do it**:
  1. Click the **Prompt Chains (🔗)** button.
  2. Create Step 1: Type *"Research the history of the Eiffel Tower."*
  3. Create Step 2: Type *"Summarize the previous output into one short paragraph."*
  4. Create Step 3: Type *"Translate the summary into Spanish."*
  5. Run the chain and watch the AI do all three steps in order!

### Example 4: Exporting a Chat to PDF
* **The Goal**: You had a great brainstorming session with the AI and want to save it to print or share with a friend.
* **How to do it**:
  1. Look at the top of your current chat.
  2. Click the **Export to PDF** button.
  3. A new tab will open with a beautifully formatted document, and your computer's print menu will pop up. Choose "Save as PDF" or select your printer.

## 5. Troubleshooting FAQ

**Q: The AI says "API Key Missing or Invalid". What do I do?**
A: You need a special password called an API Key from Venice AI. Go to the Settings (⚙️) in the sidebar, paste your key, and click Save. Make sure there are no extra spaces at the end!

**Q: I asked it to summarize a YouTube video, but it says it can't find the text.**
A: Some videos don't have captions or transcripts available. Try another video. Also, make sure you actually clicked the "Include Page Context" (📄) button before sending your message!

**Q: The sidebar won't open when I press the keyboard shortcut.**
A: Sometimes Chrome gets confused. Try clicking the extension icon (the puzzle piece) in the top right of your browser instead. You can also check your Chrome shortcut settings by going to `chrome://extensions/shortcuts`.

**Q: The AI is typing very slowly.**
A: This depends on your internet connection and how busy the Venice AI servers are. If it's stuck, you can refresh the page or start a New Chat.

## 6. Glossary of Terms

* **AI (Artificial Intelligence)**: A computer program designed to think, write, and answer questions like a human.
* **API Key**: A secret code that acts like a VIP pass, allowing your extension to talk to the Venice AI servers securely.
* **Context**: The background information you give the AI. If you tell it to "read this page," the page is the context.
* **Model**: The specific "brain" the AI uses. Some models are better at writing stories, while others are better at writing computer code. You can change models in the Settings.
* **Prompt**: The question or instruction you type to the AI. (e.g., "Write a poem about dogs" is a prompt).
* **Prompt Chain**: A series of connected prompts where the AI finishes one task and immediately uses that answer to start the next task.
* **TTS (Text-to-Speech)**: A feature that turns written text into spoken audio, like a robot reading out loud.
